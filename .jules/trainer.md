## 2025-04-19 - [Data Mapping issue]\n**Learning:** PokeAPI item IDs overlap or completely differ from in-game internal item IDs. When parsing saves for inventory items, we must map them directly to internal IDs, but PokeAPI metadata exports standard indices. Instead of parsing at runtime, we map PokeAPI item ids directly to an array of internal IDs during build time in generate-pokedata.ts\n**Action:** Replaced `item?: number` with `item?: number[]` in schema.
